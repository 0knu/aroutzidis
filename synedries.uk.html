Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about sessions

How long does a session last?

A typical session lasts about 50-60 minutes. However, the duration can be adjusted according to your needs and the type of therapy.

How many sessions do I need?

The number of sessions depends on your individual needs and goals. Some clients see improvement in a few sessions, while others may need long-term support.

Are sessions confidential?

Absolutely. All information you share with me is strictly confidential and protected by the professional code of ethics.

How can I prepare for the first session?

No special preparation is required. It's helpful to think about the reasons you want therapy and your goals. I will guide you through the entire process.

What happens if I cancel a session?

Session cancellation is accepted up to 24 hours before the scheduled start time. For a cancellation with less than the mentioned time, the cost is charged to the client/counselee. Emergency cases are an exception.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, I offer online sessions via Skype for those who prefer or need this option. The client must have an active Skype account.

How do payments work?

For online sessions, payment is made in advance. For office sessions, payment is made in cash or by card during the session. Receipts are issued for all transactions.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by phone at +30 2310 420 634 or through the contact form on my website.
